# Security policy

Report vulnerabilities privately to the security contact published by the
operating company. Do not include exchange secrets, passwords, tokens or
unnecessary personal data. Production launch must define a monitored security
address, acknowledgement target and escalation owner before onboarding users.

Include affected version/endpoint, reproducible steps, impact and mitigation.
Do not test against other customers, access unnecessary data, perform denial of
service, or disclose unresolved issues publicly without coordination.

Operators should acknowledge valid reports within two business days, preserve
evidence, communicate remediation timelines and credit reporters when requested
and legally possible. Security incidents follow the incident and breach runbooks.
