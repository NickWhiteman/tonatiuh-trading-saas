# Subprocessor governance

Maintain the customer-facing list with legal entity, service, processing
location, transfer mechanism, DPA date, deletion terms and security-review date.
The concrete vendor and region must be selected before production.

Required categories currently include cloud hosting/PostgreSQL/backups,
transactional email, YooKassa, exchanges selected by each customer, and support
systems that may receive customer data.

Before onboarding or materially changing a processor: complete security/privacy
due diligence, sign a DPA, determine roles, map international transfers, verify
deletion/export capability, update the public list and provide required notice.
Remove credentials and verify deletion when offboarding.
